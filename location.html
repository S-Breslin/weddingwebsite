Things To Do

Caves

Visit the Algarve caves. Whether you decide to choose a boat trip to see the dolphins and the caves or kayak to the caves themselves, they are definitely a sight to behold and something that we would highly recommend doing when in the area.

Slide & Splash

A water park near by.

Sand City

Sand City in Lagoa, is currently the Biggest Sand Sculpture Park in the World. It is also considered one of the best attractions in the Algarve for being unique and different.

Zoomarine

A zoological and aquatic park with lots of animals and amusements including water rides.

Golf

Play golf at Pestana Resort. The wedding party will be playing a round of golf the day before the wedding. All guests are welcome to play. Please let us know if you are interested and we will add you to the booking.

Kayaking & Pedalos

Why not go to your local beach and rent a kayak or pedalo.
