Other locations

The wedding party and close family and friends will be staying in Ferragudo. This is the area where Sean’s father has had an apartment for many years and an area dearly loved by the couple. In fact, it is where the couple got engaged. (PICTURE OF US IN SQUARE)We would invite any guests who wished, to stay here with us.

Ferragudo is a small fishing village. It is a well-kept secret and is very beautiful. Accommodation here is not abundant. Therefore we recommend booking as early as possible.

For hotels we recommend Vitor’s Village, Vila Castelo Parque. Both hotels are around an 8 mature walk to the main square. One2Seven is also very close, it is just a five minute walk from the square and Suites @ Portarade is located in the square.

Further hotels include:

  • Agua Hotels Riverside (32 minute walk/ 5 min drive from the square)
  • Caneiros Luxury House & Suites (28 min walk/ 6 min drive from the square)
  • Hotel Rural Brícia Du Mar (20 min walk/ 5 min drive)
  • Hotel Casabela (21 min walk/ 5 min drive)

If you are looking at a hotel on a map and want to know how close it is to the square, use ‘Restaurante Portarade’ as a reference.

There are also a wide variety of accommodations on Airbnb that will host from 2 guests to much bigger parties. Again, if you wish to know how close the Airbnb is to the square, use ‘Restaurante Portarade’ as a reference.

Things To Do

Caves

Visit the Algarve caves. Whether you decide to choose a boat trip to see the dolphins and the caves or kayak to the caves themselves, they are definitely a sight to behold and something that we would highly recommend doing when in the area.

Slide & Splash

A water park near by.

Sand City

Sand City in Lagoa, is currently the Biggest Sand Sculpture Park in the World. It is also considered one of the best attractions in the Algarve for being unique and different.

Zoomarine

A zoological and aquatic park with lots of animals and amusements including water rides.

Golf

Play golf at Pestana Resort. The wedding party will be playing a round of golf the day before the wedding. All guests are welcome to play. Please let us know if you are interested and we will add you to the booking.

Kayaking & Pedalos

Why not go to your local beach and rent a kayak or pedalo.
