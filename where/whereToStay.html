The wedding party and close family and friends will be staying in Ferragudo.

This is the area where Sean’s father has had an apartment for many years and an area dearly loved by the couple.

In fact, it is where the couple got engaged.

We would invite any guests who wished, to stay here with us.

Ferragudo is a small fishing village.

It is a well-kept secret and is very beautiful.

Accommodation here is not abundant. Therefore we recommend booking as early as possible.

For hotels we recommend Vitor’s Village, Vila Castelo Parque. Both hotels are around an 8 minute walk to the main square. One2Seven is also very close, it is just a five minute walk from the square and Suites @ Portarade is located in the square.

Further hotels include:

  • Agua Hotels Riverside (32 minute walk/ 5 min drive from the square)
  • Caneiros Luxury House & Suites (28 min walk/ 6 min drive from the square)
  • Hotel Rural Brícia Du Mar (20 min walk/ 5 min drive)
  • Hotel Casabela (21 min walk/ 5 min drive)

If you are looking at a hotel on a map and want to know how close it is to the square, use ‘Restaurante Portarade’ as a reference.

There are also a wide variety of accommodations on Airbnb that will host from 2 guests to much bigger parties. Again, if you wish to know how close the Airbnb is to the square, use ‘Restaurante Portarade’ as a reference.

Places to eat in Ferragudo

The couple’s favourite spot to eat is O' Velho Novo. The food is delicious and the price is very reasonable.

For lunch, you cannot beat the value for money in Port Arade and the food is always good.

Portugal is famous for their fish. Aria is the couple’s favourite Fish restaurant. A waiter will bring out the catch of the day and you can have your pick. They bring the fish out for you and debone it in front of you. A great experience.

If you are looking for something a little more up market, Sueste is for you. Probably the most expensive restaurant in Ferragudo but the fish is amazing.

The couple love brunch from Brunch in Rio. The food is fab and the interior is both cool in design and temperature.

For Pizza, the couple always return to Pizzeria Delizia. They never fail.

If Indian is your cuisine of choice then check out Sikandar Indian Cuisine. It is located in the square.

Tres Macacas is the couples favourite bar in Ferragudo.

Club Nau is a restaurant/ bar on the beach. It does nice lovely cocktails and has live music on Sundays during the summer.
